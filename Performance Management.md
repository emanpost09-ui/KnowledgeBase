This quick recall table covers all the key concepts of **Performance Management** as detailed in the source, including its objectives, the six-step cycle, the role of leadership, and the essential tools used.

### **Performance Management Recall Table**

| Category | Key Concepts and Actions |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Definition** | A continuous, structured process of setting expectations, monitoring results, and coaching to drive **consistent improvement and accountability**. |
| **Core Objectives** | • Align performance with client KPIs and business goals.<br>• Identify gaps early and provide data-based feedback.<br>• Build a culture of ownership and reward high performers. |
| **Step 1: Goal Setting** | • Define clear, measurable targets (KPIs) like AHT, CSAT, FCR, and QA.<br>• Use **SMART goals** (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound). |
| **Step 2: Monitoring** | • Track real-time data via dashboards, QA audits, and CRM/ACD reports.<br>• Review attendance, adherence logs, and VOC (Voice of Customer) results. |
| **Step 3: Analysis & Diagnosis** | • Distinguish between **skill, will, process, or system issues**.<br>• Use Root Cause Analysis (RCA) tools like **Pareto charts or Fishbone diagrams**. |
| **Step 4: Coaching & Development** | • Conduct 1:1 sessions focusing on **actionable behaviors**.<br>• Use refresher training, shadowing, role-playing, and QA calibration. |
| **Step 5: Recognition & Accountability** | • **Recognition:** Celebrate top scorers and most improved agents to motivate.<br>• **Accountability:** Implement fair Performance Improvement Plans (PIPs). |
| **Step 6: Continuous Feedback** | • Track progress post-coaching to see if metrics improved.<br>• Share insights with QA, Training, and WFM for systemic changes. |
| **Role of the Team Leader (TL)** | • Act as the frontline performance manager.<br>• Review dashboards daily and translate feedback into improvement plans.<br>• Escalate systemic issues to specialized teams (QA/Training/BA). |
| **Essential Tools** | • Scorecards and Dashboards (combined QA + KPI views).<br>• Coaching Logs and Actionable Behavior Frameworks.<br>• PIP Templates, Calibration Sessions, and Agent Self-Assessments. |
| **Ideal Outcomes** | • Higher CSAT/QA scores and reduced attrition/rework.<br>• Better alignment between all departments and **data-driven decisions** at every level. |
