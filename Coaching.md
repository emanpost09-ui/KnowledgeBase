This quick recall table summarizes the core purpose of coaching and the specific steps of the **DIRECT** coaching framework
### **Coaching Overview**

| Concept | Key Definition & Actions |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Core Goal** | Ensuring individuals are **equipped with the necessary knowledge and resources** to exceed company and client standards. |
| **Actionable Behaviors** | Identifying specific behaviors or underlying issues that are **within an individual's control** and can be acted upon. |

### **The DIRECT Coaching Method**

| Step                             | Key Actions & Strategies                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| :------------------------------- | :--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **D - Develop Rapport**          | • Build a positive, trusting relationship with open, respectful communication.<br>• Practice active, empathetic listening and show interest in their goals.<br>• Find common ground to strengthen the connection.                                    |
| **I - Indicate Focus**           | • Define coaching objectives, desired outcomes, and specific areas for development.<br>• Set clear performance goals and expectations.<br>• Ensure mutual understanding of why these focus areas are important for growth.                           |
| **R - Reinforce and Recognize**  | • Acknowledge progress and provide timely feedback on strengths and improvements.<br>• Identify milestones and celebrate achievements.<br>• Offer incentives and foster a team culture of recognition.                                               |
| **E - Engage in Self-discovery** | • Use **open-ended questions** to prompt critical thinking and self-reflection.<br>• Help individuals explore their own values, strengths, and development areas.<br>• Guide them to identify their own solutions and strategies.                    |
| **C - Close the Gap**            | • Collaboratively create a development plan using **SMART GOALS** (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound).<br>• Define action steps and provide resources to bridge the performance gap.<br>• Regularly review and assess progress. |
| **T - Tweak**                    | • Continuously monitor progress and provide ongoing feedback to fine-tune skills.<br>• Adjust the development plan based on changing priorities or circumstances.<br>• Support the individual in overcoming obstacles with flexibility.              |
| **T - Timeline**                 | • Establish a clear schedule for coaching sessions and progress reviews.<br>• Set deadlines for specific goals and milestones.<br>• Ensure the timeline is realistic for development and adjust for individual needs.                                |
